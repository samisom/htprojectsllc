About Us

Hometown Projects LLC was built in the Indianapolis area by co-owners Andrew Hargrove and Sam Isom. We both grew up in this community — going to school here, playing sports, and working our first jobs locally — which has built our deep appreciation for the neighborhoods and people we serve.

What started as a small side hustle doing landscaping to earn extra money, gradually grew into the company Hometown Projects is today. Through hard work, consistency, and a commitment to doing things the right way, we turned long days and early mornings into a business we’re proud to stand behind.

We strive to bring the highest quality of service to every project, whether it’s a simple yard refresh or a full outdoor transformation. Clear communication, dependable work, and attention to detail are the values that guide our services.

At the end of the day, we’re more than just a landscaping company — we’re locals building something meaningful in the community that raised us. We’re grateful for every customer who trusts us with their property and look forward to continuing to grow alongside the people we serve.
